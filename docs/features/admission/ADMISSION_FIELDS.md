# DepEd Early Registration & Enrollment Fields: JHS Multi-SCP Reference

**Document Version:** 5.0.0
**Institution:** Enriqueta Montilla de Esteban Memorial High School
**Scope:** Grades 7-10 (BEC, STE, SPA, SPS, SPJ, SPFL, SPTVE)

---

## 1. Dynamic Phase 1 Fields (Intake & Screening)

### 1.1 Gate 1: Prerequisites (All SCPs)
All SCP applicants must provide:
- **Grade 6 Q1/Q2 Average:** Baseline qualifying threshold (typically 85.00, based on active school configuration).
- **Program-specific documentary set:** Enforced by selected SCP path.

### 1.2 Program-Specific Prerequisites
- **STE:** Checks Math/Science/English grades; requires Medical Certificate and Good Moral Certificate.
- **SPA:** Requires sub-specialization (Visual Arts, Music, Theater, Dance, Media Arts, Creative Writing); Medical Certificate required especially for Dance.
- **SPS:** Requires selected sport, Medical Certificate, and Sports Awards/Certificates upload.
- **SPJ:** Checks English and Filipino grades; accepts publication portfolio if available.
- **SPFL:** Checks English subject grade.
- **SPTVE:** Requires Medical Certificate and Good Moral Certificate.

### 1.3 Gate 2: Assessment Scores
Recorded by SCP Coordinators or Coaches:
- **STE:** Written Exam Score (Math, Science, English).
- **SPA:** Audition / Portfolio Score.
- **SPS:** Fitness / Tryout Score.
- **SPJ:** Writing Proficiency Score.
- **SPFL:** Language Aptitude Score.
- **SPTVE:** Technical / Spatial Skills Score.

### 1.4 Gate 3: Interview & Ranking
- **Interview Score:** Logged for all SCP applicants.
- **Weighted Total:** `(Gate 2 Score * 0.6) + (Interview * 0.2) + (Q1/Q2 Avg * 0.2)`.

---

## 2. Dynamic Phase 2 Fields (Official Enrollment)

### 2.1 Academic Validation (All Learners)
- **Final General Average:** From finalized SF9.
- **Promoted Status:** Boolean toggle.

### 2.2 Form Completion
- **Full BEEF:** Capturing all 10 sections of demographics.

### 2.3 Brigada Assessments (Recorded in SIMS)
- **Reading Levels:** Independent, Instructional, Frustration, Non-Reader.
- **Health Data:** Height (cm), Weight (kg), BMI (Calculated).

---

## 3. Returning Learner Fields (G8-10)
- **Confirmation Slip:** Simplified demographic confirmation.
- **New Grade Level:** Target grade for upcoming SY.
- **Previous SY Final Average:** For sectioning ranking.
