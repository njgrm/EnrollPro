# Documentary Requirements: Enriqueta JHS Multi-SCP Framework

**Document Version:** 5.0.0
**Institution:** Enriqueta Montilla de Esteban Memorial High School
**Target Scope:** Junior High School (Grades 7-10)

---

## 1. PHASE 1: Intake & Screening (Jan – May)

All Grade 7 applicants require a **PSA Birth Certificate** (Photocopy) and the **Early Registration Form**.

### 1.1 Program-Specific Requirements (Gate 1)
| SCP Code | Additional Documents Required |
|---|---|
| **STE** | Medical Certificate, Good Moral Certificate, Certified SF9 (Partial Grades). |
| **SPA** | Medical Certificate (especially for Dance), Portfolio (Visual/Creative Writing). |
| **SPS** | Medical Certificate, Upload of "Sports Awards/Certificates". |
| **SPJ** | Portfolio of publications (School Paper/Journals) if available. |
| **SPFL** | Certified SF9 (Partial Grades). |
| **SPTVE** | Medical Certificate, Good Moral Certificate. |

---

## 2. PHASE 2: Official Enrollment (July – August)

| Category | Required Documents |
|---|---|
| **Incoming Grade 7** | Original SF9 (Finalized), Completed BEEF. |
| **Returning Learners** | Original SF9 (Finalized), DepEd Confirmation Slip. |
| **Transferees** | Original SF9 (Finalized), Completed BEEF from Enriqueta. |

---

## 3. Compliance & Exceptions

### Documentary Grace Period
If a learner lacks a **PSA Birth Certificate** or an **original SF9** in July:
- **System Action:** Tagged as `TEMPORARILY_ENROLLED`.
- **Deadline:** October 31.
- **Rule:** Failure to submit by the deadline triggers a compliance alert for the registrar and school ICT.

### Transferee Record Continuity
Upon tagging as `ENROLLED`, the SIMS module automatically initiates a digital request for **School Form 10 (SF10)** from the origin school. Parents are not tasked with this transmission.
